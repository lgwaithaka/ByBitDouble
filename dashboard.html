Entry Gates (ALL must pass)

• Volume >1.3x avg: Research: high-vol breakouts succeed 71%, low-vol fail 67%

• RSI divergence: +15 confidence bonus (74% of Week 1 winners showed divergence)

Position Management

• SL: 1.8× ATR minimum (raised from 1.2× — fixes noise-out problem)

• Breakeven SL on first profit tick: SL moves above entry (Long) / below entry (Short) the moment position is profitable — can never lose

• Tier 1 at +15%: Close 40%, SL → entry+1% (guaranteed 15% on 40%)

• Tier 2 at +20%: Close 30%, SL trails at 1.5× ATR (locks +20% on 30%)

• Tier 3 at +25%: Close remaining 30%, full market exit (full profit secured)

• Session sizing: +25% notional during 19:00–20:59 UTC peak (85% WR)

• Pyramid: +50% size at +2.5% (raised from +0.8% to avoid noise adds)

Modes

• NORMAL: 12% risk/trade, 68+ conf floor (minimum — CONSERVATIVE removed)

• AGGRESSIVE: 18% risk/trade, 62+ conf — activates at 2 wins streak

• TURBO: 25% risk/trade, 55+ conf — 80%+ epoch progress or late epoch
