Strategy Parameters

Risk Management — How It Works

• Compound sizing: As balance grows each epoch, dollar value of every position scales proportionally. $4 risk at $100 becomes $8 at $200, $16 at $400.

• Kelly fraction: Position size = balance × Kelly(win_rate) × mode_multiplier × streak_multiplier.

• Mode multipliers: CONSERVATIVE 0.5×, NORMAL 1.0×, AGGRESSIVE 1.35×, TURBO 1.65×.

• Streak multiplier: 3+ win streak = 1.12×. 3+ loss streak = 0.60×. Protects capital during losing runs.

• ATR stops: Every trade has a hard stop at sl_mult × ATR. Take profit at tp_mult × ATR (min 1:2 R:R).

• Epoch circuit breaker: Halts if balance falls 30% below epoch start. Resets at epoch boundary.

• Daily circuit breaker: Halts if daily drawdown exceeds 20%. Resets at midnight UTC.

• Notional cap: No single trade uses more than 35% of balance as margin.
