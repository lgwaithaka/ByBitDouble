📖 Strategy Rules Reference

Entry Rules

• 4H macro gate: Must confirm trend direction (ADX > 15)

• 1H structure gate: Must agree with 4H (hard veto if not)

• 24/7 continuous trading: scan volume scales with liquidity session; risk management and progressive profit protection maximise returns

• LTF veto: Block if 5m MACD + 3m cross both negative

• Fear & Greed: >80 → +10 conf for LONGs; <20 → +8 for SHORTs

• Confidence minimum: NORMAL 68 · AGGRESSIVE 62 · TURBO 55

Position Management

• 10% hold target: Positions closed at +10% profit

• Breakeven: SL moved to entry + buffer at +4%

• Trailing stop: 1.5× ATR trailing activates at +7%

• SL: 1.8× ATR behind market structure

• TP: 2.8× ATR (1:1.56 R:R minimum)

• Pyramid: +50% size added at +0.8% in AGGRESSIVE/TURBO

Risk Controls

• CB bypass: Circuit breakers only halt NORMAL mode

• Auto-blacklist: 2+ trades with 0% win rate = excluded

• Adaptive cooldown: 120s + 60s/net loss (max 600s)
